Who We Are

A New Standard in African Minerals Excellence

Apexnova Metals Ltd. — operating under the banner of Giant Africa Mining Explorations (GAME) — is a mining investment portfolio, minerals & metals commodity trading, and investment advisory company established in 2022.

"We hold mining prospecting and minerals trading rights across six strategic sites in Zimbabwe, supplemented by procurement partnerships across Zambia and the DRC."

From exploration to export, we deliver top-tier concentrates and ores to corporate buyers and high-net-worth traders across the Americas, Asia, and Europe. Our operations are anchored by cutting-edge technology, internationally certified quality control, and an unwavering commitment to safe, sustainable, and ethical business practices.

We create measurable value for our employees, customers, shareholders, and the local communities in which we operate.

What We Do

End-to-End Mining Services

From prospecting licence to port delivery — we manage the full mineral supply chain with precision and accountability.

01
Exploration & Prospecting
We hold active prospecting licences across six strategic sites in Zimbabwe. Our geological teams deploy advanced survey technologies to identify and quantify mineral resources with high accuracy.
02
Minerals Procurement
We procure top-grade minerals and metals directly from licensed producers in Zimbabwe, Zambia, and the DRC — ensuring traceable, conflict-free sourcing at every stage.
03
Quality Assurance & Certification
All products are independently assayed and certified by internationally recognised bodies including SGS, CCIC, RC Inspection, Mitra SK, and Alex Stewart International — guaranteeing zero-defect deliveries.
04
Commodities Trading
Our experienced trading desk facilitates seamless transactions between producers and international buyers, with transparent pricing benchmarked to global commodity markets.
05
Logistics & Export
We coordinate efficient, fully documented transit from mine to port to buyer. Strategic logistics partnerships ensure prompt delivery to markets across the Americas, Asia, and Europe.
06
Investment Advisory
We advise corporate and high-net-worth clients on mining investment opportunities across Southern and Central Africa, providing due diligence, valuation, and deal structuring services.
